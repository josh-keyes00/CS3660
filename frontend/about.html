About Wizz Wizards Worldwide

At Wizz Wizards Worldwide, we exist to meet every wizarding need imaginable — from powerful enchantments and world-shaping spells to potions that can heal, empower, or (accidentally) turn your mayor into a frog. As the leading wizarding company in the world, we pride ourselves on delivering magical solutions that are as reliable as they are spectacular. Whether you’re a kingdom in crisis or a small adventuring party needing a boost, Wizz Wizards Worldwide is here to provide enchantment, spellcraft, and alchemy of the highest order.

Meet the Founders

Professor Archibald Percival the Seventeenth of Lower Mossbottomshire – The legendary Spell Master of Wizz Wizards Worldwide. Archibald has seen more battles than most libraries have books, including leading the defense against the Invasions of the Nine Hells where he personally banished three balor generals back to their fiery pits. His spell mastery ranges from apocalyptic firestorms to intricate protective wards, and his combat record includes slaying no fewer than seven dragons. Today, he ensures that our spellmaking services remain unmatched, from simple charms to reality-warping incantations.

Sir Bartholomew Von Quagmire III, Keeper of the Sacred Binder Clips – Our Enchanting Specialist. Sir Bartholomew has enchanted everything from swords to quills to entire castles — and on one famous occasion, the royal outhouse (don’t ask). During the Siege of Silverkeep, he enchanted an army’s armor to sparkle so brightly it blinded the enemy into surrender. With centuries of enchanting expertise, Bartholomew ensures that Wizz Wizards Worldwide delivers magical enhancements that dazzle, protect, and inspire.

Dame Esmeralda Fantasia Sprocket of the Glittering Marshes – The Potion Master Supreme. Esmeralda has brewed everything from elixirs that grant the strength of ten giants to potions that can cure plagues in a single sip. She once fueled an entire dwarven workforce for twelve straight years with her infamous Elixir of Eternal Mondays. During the Dragon Wars, her volatile concoctions brought down wyverns mid-flight. At Wizz Wizards Worldwide, she heads potion development, ensuring our brews are both legendary and—mostly—safe.

Our Legacy

With more than 1,600 years of combined experience, the founders of Wizz Wizards Worldwide have slain dragons, thwarted infernal invasions, and revolutionized magical business practices. Beyond their legendary feats, they have forged lasting partnerships with kingdoms across the globe, providing wizarding services at the highest level — from royal courts to remote villages in need of protection. Together, they have built a company trusted by kings, adventurers, and everyday folk alike, setting the gold standard for magical excellence worldwide.

Wizz Wizards Worldwide isn’t just the best in the industry — we are the industry. From enchantments to spellmaking to potion mastery, we don’t just provide magic — we set the standard for wizarding excellence across the world.
